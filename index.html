Edvin Ryding

Edvin Ryding or Lars Edvin Folke Ryding is a Swedish actor. He was born on February 4, 2003 in Östermalm, Stockholm, Stockholms län, Sweden.

He debuted in the TV series Mannen under trappan in 2009 at the age of 6 years. He is an actor, known for Young Royals (2021), Fröken Frimans krig (2013) and Gåsmamman (2015).

He became known internationally with the leading role in the 2021 Netflix show Young Royals, where he plays the character Prince Wilhelm. Ryding was featured in Europe's Forbes 30 under 30 list of 2022.

Edvin has won awards from the Stockholm International Film Festival (2021), Gaygalan Awards (2022), Kristallen (2022), and Gaygalan Awards (2023).
